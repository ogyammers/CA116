Work Experience

Since 2014 I have worked for a bar and restaurant group called NolaClan and specifically in a Bar called House Dublin, One of the best venues in the city. In House, I started as a cellarman which involved the taking in of deliveries and stocking the bars with the necessary goods and perishables needed to run smoothly

I then got promoted to waiting tables, where I learned a lot about drinks, food, and people, particularly social skills and people skills that have been invaluable to me so far in life.

After this, I found a true passion when promoted to bartender. I enjoyed learning and mastering the technical skills required of bartending in a fast-paced, high-volume, and high standards environment. I went on to represent House in many cocktail competitions.

Before returning to College this year, I served as Bar supervisor and a part of the management team.


While Bars and restaurants were closed due to covid, I worked on a building site as a general operative. This work consisted of assisting trades, tidying the site, and moving materials around. The site was One Wilton, a 150,000sqm development which is pre-let to Linkedln.
